I am PhD candidate at ETH Zürich under the supervision of Gunnar Rätsch member of the Biomedical Informatics group

I work on the in silico prediction of tumor neoepitopes and their relationship with the immune compartment for immunotherapy. I am currently developing a software tool, to integrate RNA and DNA alterations into a graph representation and generate candidate neoepitopes. I also work in collaboration with A. Nellore- R. Thompson Lab at OHSU to ensure algorithmic reproducibility of the neoepitoes generation and differential filtering. I also aim at developing novel approaches to search rare neoepitope candidates in proteomic databases and develop a predictive model of validation. I plan to use the software tool to make biological discoveries. The former, combined with data analytics techniques will allow the stratification of patients and the prediction of immunotherapy outcomes in relationship with their omics status.
